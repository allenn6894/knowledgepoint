# SSC CGL 2026

## Overview

Staff Selection Commission (SSC) Combined Graduate Level (CGL) is a national-level recruitment examination for graduates seeking government posts. The exam is typically conducted in multiple tiers and requires consistent preparation across quantitative aptitude, reasoning, English, and general awareness.
