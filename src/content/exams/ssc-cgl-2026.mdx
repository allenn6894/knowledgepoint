# SSC CGL 2026

## About CGL

Staff Selection Commission (SSC) Combined Graduate Level (CGL) is a national-level recruitment examination conducted for graduate-level Group B and Group C posts across various ministries, departments, and organizations of the Government of India. The exam is conducted in multiple tiers and requires consistent preparation across quantitative aptitude, reasoning, English, and general awareness.
