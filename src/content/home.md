# Knowledge Point Overview

This frontend template is designed for a knowledge portal with:

- blog posts
- video collections
- a flexible menu bar
- markdown-driven content

Update the **menu items** and **content pages** quickly by editing the files under `src/data/pages.js` and `src/content/`.

## Why this template?

- Easy to extend with new sections
- Simple to manage content in markdown
- Clean, responsive layout for learning resources

Navigate using the menu to explore blog and video sections.
